E-Tabs
Most successful Software in Civil Engineering for Design & Analysis. E-Tabs & Tall buildings is a two-day Workshop on Designing a Building and Exposure to E-tabs with Hand-so me Practical session followed by Wind based Design and Seismic Design Resonance spectrum Analysis & Push Over Analysis.

BIM-REVIT
Most successful software in civil engineering for design and visualization, simulation and construction. Revit is a two day workshop on BIM in construction management which includes building the models with some case studies on industrial projects.
Event Details

X
  • Course Highlights:
    Building Information Modelling-Introduction
    BIM in Construction Management
    Interpretation of BIM and benefits of BIM
  • Course Content:
    Virtual Prototyping of Models
    Practical Session (using Industry-grade software)
    Mass Modeling with Morph tool
    Solid Element Operations and Creation of sections, elevations
    Modelling the Terrain-Creating Site
    Creating various structural elements with preloaded data
    Generate 3D documents from Floor Plans
    Building the models with sections, elevations etc.
    Refining Surfaces and Publishing
    Usage of Profiled Elements and Rendering
    Case studies on Industrial projects
  • Certification:
    Certificate of Completion.
    Certificate of Completion with Distinction (for top performers).
  • Eligibility:
    This workshop is recommended for Civil Engineering students interested to practically learn Building Information Modelling(BIM).
