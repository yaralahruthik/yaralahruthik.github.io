The Institution & The Department

Jawaharlal Nehru Technological University Hyderabad was established in 1965.The college offers various courses in Engineering and Technology. The students are being placed in reputed organizations through the campus placements. It has got an Entrepreneurship Development Cell to encourage and promote the students to become job providers instead of job seekers.

The Department of Civil Engineering is one of the departments existing from the inception of the college. At present the department is offering B.Tech regular and Integrated dual degree program, M.Tech regular and part-time. The department is rated as the best department by the state committee organized by government of Telangana. The department has been accredited by NBA committee.
